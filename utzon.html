Architecture, Light and Living Design

Ongoing:

"I am Robot"

Price:

The Utzon Center in Aalborg celebrates architecture, design, and creative thinking, inspired by Jørn Utzon, the architect of Sydney Opera House. Located by the waterfront, it hosts exhibitions, and workshops that explore how architecture shapes life, blending design ideas with a strong local and cultural context.
