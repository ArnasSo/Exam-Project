Year

Mural Name

“Liqen”

Liqen was born in Spain in 1980 and has been painting since he was a child. He is very interested in people and nature, and the diversity of species in particular has a central place in his consciousness. When Liqen decorates, it is under an overall theme, about the way we treat the environment and cultures. He has painted gable paintings in Mexico, Italy, England and the USA and now finally in Denmark.
