NORDKRAFT

A former power plant turned cultural hotspot.

Opening Hours:

Monday - Friday: 10:00-18:00

Saturday - Sunday: 10:00-18:00

Nordkraft is a key cultural hub located in a former power station, reflecting the city's shift from industrial roots to creative life. Since opening in 2009, it has brought together art, music, theatre, cinema, and community activities, making it an important space for local culture and creative expression.
